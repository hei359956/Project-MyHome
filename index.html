Fast Shipping

Most signs and canvas prints will ship via FedEx or UPS Ground service which typically has a 1-5 day transit time. Please refer to the color coded map below to check the transit time to your area. Smaller items may be shipped via USPS first class or priority mail, with varying delivery times. You will receive an email containing tracking information after your order ships.

The shipping rates quoted on our website are for domestic shipments to the lower 48 states only. Shipping rates to Alaska and Hawaii will likely be higher, so please contact us before ordering if shipping to either of these states. International shipping is available on select items. Please contact us for a shipping quote for any order outside the United States. If an international order is placed without first contacting us we will send an email including the additional shipping cost along with the option to proceed or cancel the order.
